# Magpie Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 11 July 2026_

Magpie is built to keep your plans private.

## What we store, and where

Your calendars, events, comments, labels, and profile live on your device and
in your personal iCloud account (Apple's private CloudKit database). We do
not run our own servers and cannot read your data.

## Sharing

When you share a calendar, its events and comments become visible to the
people you invite, through Apple's iCloud sharing. You can remove members or
stop sharing at any time.

## Sign in with Apple

Magpie uses your Apple ID as your identity, so there are no passwords. Apple
shares your name (and email, if you choose) on your first sign-in; it is
stored on your device and used only to label your profile and comments.

## Analytics and ads

Magpie shows no adverts and contains no third-party trackers. Anonymous usage
counts (for example, "an event was created") are logged only on your device
and never include titles, names, dates, or anything you type.

## Notifications

Daily digests and reminders are scheduled entirely on your device from your
own calendar data.

## Payments

Magpie Plus purchases are processed by Apple. We never see your payment
details.

## Your choices

You can delete your account in Settings, which permanently removes all
Magpie data from your device and your iCloud. Contact us with any questions
via the App Store listing, or email zach.mitchell1@outlook.com.

## Children

Magpie is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect
information from anyone.

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at this
