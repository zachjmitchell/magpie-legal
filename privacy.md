# Magpie Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 4 July 2026_

Magpie is a shared calendar app for iOS, made by Zach Mitchell ("we", "us").
This policy explains what happens to your information when you use Magpie.
The short version: **your data belongs to you, lives in your own Apple
accounts, and we never see it.**

## What Magpie stores, and where

- **Calendars, events, comments, and labels** you create are stored on your
  device and, when iCloud sync is available, in **your own private iCloud
  database**. We have no server of our own and no ability to read this data.
- **Shared calendars** are shared through Apple's iCloud sharing. Only the
  people you invite can see a shared calendar's events and comments.
- **Your profile** (display name, avatar colour, optional profile photo) is
  stored on your device. Your profile photo never leaves your device.
- **Optional calendar background photos** are stored on your device only.

## Sign in with Apple

Magpie uses Sign in with Apple to create your account. We receive only what
Apple provides: an anonymous account identifier, and — if you choose to share
them on first sign-in — your name and email address. These are used solely to
set up your profile inside the app. We do not send them anywhere.

## Apple Calendar import

If you use "Import from Apple Calendar", Magpie reads events from your Apple
Calendar with your permission and copies them into Magpie. Apple Calendar is
never modified, and the imported events are handled like any other Magpie
data (on device and in your iCloud).

## Purchases

Magpie Plus subscriptions and the lifetime purchase are processed entirely by
Apple through the App Store. We never see your payment details.

## What we collect

Nothing. Magpie contains no third-party analytics, no advertising, and no
tracking. Aggregate download and sales statistics are provided to us by Apple
and contain no personal information.

## Data deletion

You can delete everything at any time in **Settings → Delete Account** inside
the app. This permanently removes all your calendars, events, and comments
from your device and from your iCloud database. Deleting the app from your
device also removes all locally stored data.

## Children

Magpie is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect
information from anyone.
