# Magpie Support

Need help with Magpie? Start here.

## Common questions

**How do I share a calendar?**
Go to the Calendars tab, open a calendar, and tap "Invite people." Invites
are sent as iCloud links through Messages, WhatsApp, email, or however you'd
like to share them.

**I signed in before but Magpie is asking me to create an account again.**
Use "Log In" on the welcome screen instead of "Create Account," and sign in
with the same Apple ID you used originally. Your calendars sync back
automatically.

**How do I cancel Magpie Plus?**
On your device, go to Settings → your name → Subscriptions → Magpie Plus,
and cancel there. Deleting the app does not cancel a subscription.

**Notifications aren't arriving.**
Check Settings → Notifications inside Magpie, and make sure notifications
are allowed for Magpie in your device's system Settings.

**I found a bug, or have a suggestion.**
Email us, we read everything.
