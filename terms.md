Magpie Plus is available as a monthly or yearly auto-renewing subscription.

- Payment is charged to your Apple ID account at confirmation of purchase.

- Manage or cancel your subscription in your device Settings → your name →
  Subscriptions. Deleting the app does NOT cancel a subscription.
- When a subscription lapses you keep all your data; Plus features simply
  lock again.
